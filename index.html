About

Work

As a Northeastern student, I will have the opportunity to participate in three 6-month co-op positions. I recently began my second co-op as a .Net Co-op at Genuine Interactive.

For my first co-op I spent 6 months at a small startup named Viridian Spark. Viridian Spark was an implementer of Content Management Systems(CMS). A CMS is an application that allows clients to update their website without requiring the involvement of a web developer. At Viridian Spark, I was a member of the back-end development team. My duties included the creation of data templates and html renderings, bug fixing and support, daily scrums with the client, and end of sprint presentations of the work I had built.

In previous summers (I think the count is up to 7) I worked as a Programming/Product Management Intern at Aras Corporation. My responsibilities at Aras varied each summer. I led development of two separate Silverlight applications. The first was a timesheet application to be used by internal contractors. The second was a CRM designed for use by Aras’s partners. While at Aras, I also contributed to the development of a custom CMS platform built on Aras’s Innovator system. Most recently, I was involved in the reengineering of the Aras Community Forums. Originally based on a third-party system, the forums were redesigned to be driven by Innovator as well.
